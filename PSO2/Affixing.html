Quick Affixing Formulas

Formulas

Phail and I came up with these basic formulas that offer good all around stats for somewhat cheap prices.
I do recommend playing around with the affix builder because the market can make some parts scarce or make other alternatives better.

Range-Mitra Formula
Range-Shiva Formula
Mitra Formula 2(90% on non-boost week)

These two formulas give the same result basically, but as you can tell, theres a few different ways to make it cheaper.
So, taking this apart, we have some basic affixes, a capsule and a SAF Precision V.
The SAF comes from a +10 novel unit which you can get from the Rising Weapon Badge 5 shop (RWB5) shop.

The Glares all offer different stats depending on the boss:
Origin glare can work for all 3 damage types (at the cost of 10 DEF)
Mitra Glare is for Melee and Range
Varuna for Melee and Tech
Shiva for Range and Tech

The Persona Reveries come from Persona UQ and the Ultimate Quest to Kill Masq.

Elegant Stamina comes from a capsule called "Augment (HP & PP/2)".
Think of capsules as an addon affix that doesn't need fodder and can be safely added at 100%.
You can swap it out for any other capsule like HP & PP/3 or the range capsule Range & PP/3 for example.
I wouldn't recommend grand range or other expensive capsules as you can put that on a much better build.

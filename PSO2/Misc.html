Room Explanations

Locked Room - Password and limit parties are the only settings that block people from joining a room.
Alliance/Friends only affect only YOUR party.
Party Commands - remove the password and hit apply changes.
It can take a bit to update for matchmaking. Alliance only doesn't do anything but block non-alliance from joining your party only.
Joining Locked Rooms - Yellow highlights = trigger quests, shields are alliance members. You have to be on the same block as them to join

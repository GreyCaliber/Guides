Leveling Methods

The road to 85...

General Leveling Tips

You can stack different boosters like Triboosts, exp boosts, rdr boosts on top of each other. So, a 100% tri boost(given out from events), 75% exp boost(photon sphere shop), and 250% rdr boost(excube shop) all stack additively.
If you are in an alliance, you can get a 10% multiplicative exp buff from going to the tree at alliance quarters and another 10% from an attack drink or premium drinks at the drink machine. The non-premium drink rolls are random, but 10% exp is possible on the second buff of the attack drink.

Leveling 1-100

1-80

Focus on getting a subclass and mag first.
Do Recommended Quest 4 which cycles through several variations daily. Castle, piper and broken shadows are the best exp variations. If there is a boosted special quest running, it may be best to run that instead.
Sub Classes only level up to Levelcap - 20 from the exp split. You’ll have to make your sub class the main class to level it past 80.

80-100

Keys become the quickest and best way to level at lvl 80 since enemies stop leveling at 80. Also, once you hit 80 on a subclass, swap it out for another if you are leveling your main class. That saves exp from being wasted on the 80 subclass. I recommended leveling the subclass with keys first as weapons are class specific.
Once you hit 85/85, you can start running UH Urgent quests too which gives decent exp. Just make sure you have decent gear so you aren’t killed constantly.
Once your main class/subclass are ready, get every other non-scion class to 75 for stat boosts from titles.

Mag Raising

Check out the class guides and skill tree sheet for what mags are recommended. It's typically recommended to get a full 200 attack stat mag usually. Braver, Bouncer and Scion classes have skills that convert their mag’s attack stats to their class stats.

Dex classes(Phantom, Braver, Bouncer) convert dex mags to stats, while the other scion classes do any attack stat to all three attack stats. Dex classes' mag conversion skill are the only ones that add stats. The other scion classes just do conversions without adding stats to the other stats.

For example, a braver(dex into melee/range) with the sub class phantom(dex into all) converts the 200 dex mag into a total of 400 melee, 400 range and 200 tech.
A Ra(no mag skill)/Etoile (Melee/Range/Tech into those 3) would be 200 range into 200 range, 200 melee, 200 tech.

If you need to fix a mags stats, you can do it at the excube shop once its past 100 total stats. Other than that, you can raise a mag via the stat's weapons and lowering dex via mates/ furniture/sweets.
For furniture/mates/sweets, 4* is melee, 5* is RNG and 6* is TEC. Dex is just skill discs and crafted discs give significantly more dex.
Furniture can be bought fairly cheap at the furniture shop beneath the Cafe’s Entrance in the shopping plaza.

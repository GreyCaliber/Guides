Gearing Up!

From one shot to being two shot.

Units

A basic progression path is:
Whatever you pick up -> Story units/lvl 75 title units -> Cradle Units/Schvelle from rainbow keys -> Other 13* -> Klauz Set
Check the 13* unit stat sheet for an idea of the stat spread. Kras is unique and has different stat spreads for for each piece(arms has most hp of all units, while back has most attack for example).
The 13* units from cradle have a set bonus and the arms/legs have a wide variety of stat ranges, but it's roughly on par with schvelle.
If you can get good affixes, the stats can beat some 13* units that aren’t affixed.

Weapons

Rarity is very important as it influences damage range. Basically, 7 stars and higher means you do 90-100% of your dmg each hit, while lower is 10-100%.
Potentials are also important. Some class guides may offer more in depth information for your classes.
A general progression list is:
Whatever you can get/equip.-> Croesus from the collection folders -> Agile from Zieg -> Klauz

Starting with Croesus, take a look into s-grade augments as they start to make up a decent portion of your dmg/utility. I don’t recommend atlas/trailblazer weapons as the UQs aren’t in the current main rotation and the croesus is roughly on par with them. I do recommend making them for the SAFs, but you can probably ask someone in your alliance if they can toss you a copy cheap/free. Rivalate is also great, but also expensive to make in comparison to agile.

Misc

Once you’ve got your units/weapons, you can enhance them at the item scientist to +10 for units and +35 for weapons for a lot of extra stats.
Some good ways to enhance weapons is to use +30 13*/15* weapons from the collection folder.
You can also use the excube(not the best use of excubes, but it works)/photon booster shop for mini exp weapons.
If you bought an Agile, it starts initially at +30 capped.
You can raise it by enhancement caps, copies of said weapon or buying enhancement caps at the class cube shop(not recommended unless you have a 1000 lying around).
